How Did The Team Get Here?

Made from scratch

The encouraged to create Project: Guardan to help students, to be the guardian they need. often we see students struggle with their academics. we were inspired to provide them a helping hand in their endevours. a platform to share, to guide and to inform so that it could provide ease and convenience to them.

With hard work and dedication, the team is excited to soon present the project in hopes to make a lasting impact on the students of Adventsist Universtiy of the Philippines and hopes that the platform will be used for years to come.
